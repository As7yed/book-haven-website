Community Involvement

Literacy Programs

We partner with local schools and libraries to promote literacy in our community. Through book donations, reading workshops, and tutoring support, we help ensure every child has access to quality reading materials and develops a lifelong love of books.

Book Drives

Book Haven hosts quarterly book drives to collect gently used books for community centers, shelters, and hospitals. We believe everyone deserves access to books, and our drives have collected over 5,000 books for those in need in our community.
